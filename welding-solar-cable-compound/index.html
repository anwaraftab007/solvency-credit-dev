With a legacy of innovation and excellence as a leading cable compound manufacturer in Delhi NCR, DEV Polymer delivers high-quality compounds that not only meet but exceed national and international standards. Our Cable Compound Range

Each compound we manufacture is the result of rigorous R&D, carefully selected materials and stringent quality controls to ensure optimal performance across multiple use-cases. Explore our high-performance compound range below:

a) Welding Cable Compound (NBR): Our Welding Cable Compound, based on Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR), is designed for applications where mechanical robustness and thermal endurance are essential. Welding cables operate in challenging environments where exposure to oil, heat, abrasion and constant movement is the norm. The right insulation not only protects the cable but also enhances safety, flexibility and service life.

At DEV Polymer, our NBR welding cable compound is formulated to deliver exceptional oil resistance, flame retardancy and mechanical toughness, ensuring the cable retains its performance in both industrial and mobile welding operations.

Applications

  • Portable welding equipment
  • Industrial welding cables
  • Construction site welding systems
  • Fabrication workshops and shipbuilding
  • Heavy-duty mechanical environments

The robust performance of our welding cable compound ensures a longer service life, minimized maintenance and reliable power transmission under extreme mechanical and thermal stress.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Q1: What makes DEV Polymer's welding cable compound different from others in the market?
    Ans: Our NBR-based welding cable compound is developed to offer superior flexibility, heat resistance and mechanical durability, even in high-load industrial environments. Each batch is tested for flame resistance, tensile strength and oil resistance, ensuring a reliable insulation layer that performs under stress.
  • Q2: How do your solar cable compounds ensure long-term UV resistance?
    Ans: We incorporate high-performance UV stabilizers and weather-resistant additives into our solar cable compounds. These materials have been field-tested and lab-verified to withstand prolonged sun exposure without degradation, cracking or colour fading.
  • Q3: Is your welding cable compound compatible with multi-strand copper conductors?
    Ans: Yes. It is optimized for use with multi-stranded, fine-wire copper conductors, ensuring tight adhesion, ease of extrusion and flexibility for coiling, uncoiling and dragging during industrial use.
  • Q4: Can DEV Polymer develop customized compounds for specific cable types or conditions?
    Ans: Absolutely. Our in-house R&D team works closely with cable manufacturers to customize formulations based on processing conditions, application environments or unique performance criteria. We also support prototyping and trial production.
  • Q5: Are your compounds environmentally friendly or recyclable?
    Ans: We are committed to sustainability. Many of our compounds are RoHS-compliant, REACH-compliant and free from heavy metals and restricted substances. We also support our clients with formulations that align with green manufacturing and recycling goals.
