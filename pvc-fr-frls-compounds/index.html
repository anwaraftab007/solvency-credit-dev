Our Flame-Retardant PVC Compounds Range

Each compound we manufacture is developed through extensive research, precise material selection and strict quality control processes to ensure superior performance across a variety of applications. Explore our high-performance compound range below.

a) PVC FR Compound: Our PVC FR Compound is one of the most reliable insulation materials for applications where fire safety is paramount. Owing to its high chlorine content, PVC inherently resists combustion. It self-extinguishes in the absence of an ignition source, making it a trusted material for fire-sensitive installations.

Our PVC FR compounds are meticulously formulated using high-grade additives and stabilizers to provide consistent flame resistance without sacrificing flexibility, tensile strength, or processing ease. They do not support flame propagation under normal atmospheric conditions unless loaded with excessive flammable plasticizers, which we avoid through expert formulation control.

Applications

  • House wiring and electrical cables
  • Industrial wire and cable systems
  • Electric insulating mats and panels
  • Consumer appliances requiring fire-safe insulation

Our PVC FR compound is ideal for manufacturers seeking an affordable yet reliable insulation solution that meets the increasing demands for fire-safe electrical products.

b) PVC FRLS Compound: In high-occupancy environments like residential towers, hospitals, commercial complexes and transportation hubs, controlling smoke emission during a fire can mean the difference between life and death. Thatâ€™s where our PVC FRLS compound comes in.

FRLS stands for Flame Retardant Low Smoke and it refers to specially formulated PVC compounds that prevent the spread of fire while producing minimal toxic smoke and low halogen emissions. In the event of a fire, cables insulated with FRLS compounds reduce the risk of smoke inhalation and visibility obstruction, enabling faster evacuation and safer fire control operations.

Our PVC FRLS compounds are formulated with halogen-free additives, advanced smoke suppressants and high-performance plasticizers to meet the most stringent fire safety standards across India.

Applications

  • Residential buildings and high-rises
  • Commercial complexes and shopping malls
  • Airports, metro stations and public infrastructure
  • Industrial plants, refineries and control rooms
  • Educational institutions and healthcare facilities
