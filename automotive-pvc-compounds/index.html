Automobile PVC Compounds for High-Performance Automotive Wiring

Home Blog

Engineered by DEV Polymer for the Future of Automotive Cabling

DEV Polymer has earned its reputation as a trusted leader in manufacturing high-performance PVC compounds for the wire and cable industry. As a leading PVC compound manufacturer in Delhi NCR, we deliver tailor-made solutions that meet the highest benchmarks of safety, durability, and regulatory compliance.

Our specialized Automobile Compound Range caters to the critical needs of automotive wiring, offering enhanced flame resistance, low smoke emission, oil resistance, and thermal stability. Built for both traditional and modern vehicles, including EVs, our FRLY compounds provide long-lasting protection and efficiency even under harsh operational conditions.

Our Automotive PVC Compound Product Range

The need for safer, more efficient and eco-conscious automotive materials has never been more pressing. At DEV Polymer, we’ve responded with a range of automotive-grade PVC insulation compounds that address these needs without compromise.

a) FRLY-B Automobile PVC Compound: FRLY-B is a specialized PVC compound developed specifically for flame-retardant, low-smoke and oil-resistant automotive cabling. Designed for low-tension electrical systems, this compound delivers consistent electrical insulation and structural integrity even under the thermal and mechanical stress common in automotive environments.

This compound is ideal for automotive wire harnesses that must endure frequent engine heating and cooling cycles. It offers remarkable resistance to oil, abrasion and thermal degradation, providing a dependable solution for internal vehicle wiring. What sets FRLY-B apart is its excellent thermal stability, low shrinkage value and consistent dielectric properties even when exposed to fluctuating operating conditions.

Its temperature ranges from -40°C to 85°C ensures year-round performance in both frigid and high-heat conditions, making it highly suitable for engine bay wiring and low-voltage circuits that require both safety and longevity.

Applications

  • Low tension electrical wiring for automobiles
  • Wiring systems in motorcycles and scooters
  • Vehicle circuits used for starting, charging and lighting
  • Instrument panel and dashboard wiring

FRLY-B offers manufacturers a cost-effective yet high-performance solution for traditional combustion-based automotive electrical systems, ensuring long-term performance without compromising on safety or processing efficiency.

b) FRLY-C High-Performance PVC Compound for EV and Advanced Vehicles: Engineered for the next generation of vehicles, FRLY-C is a high-performance, halogen-free PVC compound developed for flame retardancy, low smoke emission and superior resistance to oil and heat. As electric vehicles and high-efficiency combustion engines become more prevalent, wiring systems are exposed to harsher conditions, tighter spaces and higher power densities. FRLY-C is formulated to meet these evolving demands.

With an extended temperature tolerance of -40°C to 105°C, FRLY-C delivers robust insulation performance across an expanded thermal operating window. It is especially well-suited to electric vehicles, where higher operating temperatures and compact electrical layouts require materials with uncompromising mechanical and thermal stability.

Unlike conventional PVC compounds, FRLY-C offers outstanding retention of physical properties even after prolonged exposure to thermal cycling, mechanical stress and chemical contact.

Applications

  • Electric vehicle power and signal wiring
  • Motorcycle and two-wheeler wiring harnesses
  • Circuits for starting, charging and lighting systems
  • Instrumentation and dashboard electrical wiring
  • Applications requiring high flexibility and thermal endurance

FRLY-C is an ideal insulation solution for automotive manufacturers transitioning toward cleaner, high-efficiency transportation systems. Its halogen-free composition ensures compliance with emerging environmental norms, while its superior thermal and mechanical properties make it a top choice for EV cable manufacturers.

Industries We Serve

  • Passenger and commercial vehicle manufacturing
  • Two-wheeler and motorcycle production
  • Electric vehicle cable manufacturing
  • Industrial machinery and transport systems
  • Aftermarket automotive wiring and cable replacement

Whether building a next-generation EV or a durable scooter for Indian roads, DEV Polymer provides PVC insulation materials that guarantee performance, safety, and ease of processing.

The DEV Polymer Advantage

DEV Polymer brings a deep understanding of the challenges faced by modern cable manufacturers, particularly in the automotive sector, where safety, reliability and long-term durability are non-negotiable. Our FRLY compounds are formulated with precision to ensure high processability, superior insulation and consistent performance even in harsh and real-world conditions.

  • Deep Industry Expertise: With over 20 years of collective experience in PVC compounding, our team understands the intricacies of automotive electrical systems and the evolving standards for thermal, electrical and environmental performance.
  • Custom Formulation Capability: We offer tailor-made insulation solutions to meet specific cable designs, machinery compatibility and processing preferences. Whether you need flexibility, thermal resistance or custom colour coding, we deliver exactly what your project demands.
  • Stringent Quality Control: Every batch of compound undergoes rigorous in-house testing to ensure conformity to national and international standards for flame retardancy, dielectric strength, mechanical robustness and thermal stability.
  • Scalable Production Capacity: From pilot-scale batches for testing to large-volume production for OEM supply chains, our flexible manufacturing infrastructure can scale as per your business requirements, without compromising on consistency.

Sustainability and Safety

At DEV Polymer, we believe that high performance should not come at the cost of environmental safety. Our halogen-free formulations, low-emission compounds and waste-minimizing production practices contribute to a more sustainable automotive industry. We continuously invest in research and material innovation to ensure our products align with the future of clean mobility.
